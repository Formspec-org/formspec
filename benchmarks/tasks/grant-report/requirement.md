# Tribal CSBG Annual Report (Short Form)

Build the short-form version of the CSBG Tribal Annual Report, the yearly program report a tribal grantee submits to the federal funder. It should collect the grantee's identifying information (agency name, EIN, tribal nation served, contact person), the reporting period (start and end dates), total funds received and expended for the year, and counts of individuals and families served across the program's main service categories — things like employment assistance, education, income management, housing, emergency services, and health/social services. Dollar amounts should be validated as non-negative currency and the "expended" figures must not exceed the "received" figure. The report should be exportable to the funder's grant-data format. A shared theme is used across the short and long variants of this report, and a changelog tracks version history — but this task is only the short form.
