# Federal Grant Application

We need a full grant application form for a federal program. It should be a multi-page wizard that walks the applicant through eligibility, organization info, project narrative, budget, attachments, and a final review. Before the main form opens, a short eligibility screener determines whether the applicant qualifies — some applicants should be told they can't apply and exit there. The organization section needs legal name, EIN, website, a primary and optional secondary contact (both contacts share the same shape — name, title, email, phone). The budget should be a line-item list with cost category, description, and amount, plus automatic subtotals by category and a grand total; the amount requested elsewhere on the form must match the budget grand total. Subcontractor costs cannot exceed 49% of the total budget — that's a federal rule and must be enforced. The form should support responses that are in-progress, completed, or amended, and it should have a changelog describing the jump from v1.0 to v1.1. It should be exportable to the agency's required JSON, CSV, and XML formats. Also provide a print-friendly theme for the PDF version submitted on paper.
