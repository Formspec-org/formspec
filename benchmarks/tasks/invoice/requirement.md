# Supplier Invoice

I need a form that captures a supplier invoice end to end. It should collect the supplier's name, contact email, and address, plus the invoice number and date. The core of the form is a list of line items — each with a description, a quantity, and a unit price — and the respondent should be able to add between 1 and 20 lines. For each line, the form should automatically calculate the line total (quantity × unit price). Once lines are entered, the form should show the invoice subtotal, apply a tax rate (entered as a percentage), add any discount, and calculate the grand total. Finally, there needs to be a check that the invoice total the supplier typed matches the total the form computed from the line items — if they disagree, flag it as an error. The completed invoice should be exportable as a CSV for accounting.
