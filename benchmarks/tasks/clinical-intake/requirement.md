# Patient Intake

We're building a clinical intake form a patient fills out before their appointment. It should start with a short screener that decides whether the patient is routed to the full intake or asked to call the office — someone under 18, or with severe chest pain right now, should be sent to call instead of continuing online. If the patient continues, collect their demographics (name, date of birth, contact info including phone and email, and preferred pronouns), their insurance details, the reason for visit, and a medical history section. The medical history should allow the patient to list any ongoing conditions, and for each condition list the medications they're taking for it — so a repeatable list of conditions, each with its own repeatable list of medications. Drug names should be suggested from a standard list, and primary care provider should be picked from a directory. Any fields that aren't relevant based on earlier answers shouldn't disappear from saved data, just be hidden. The form spans multiple pages as a wizard.
