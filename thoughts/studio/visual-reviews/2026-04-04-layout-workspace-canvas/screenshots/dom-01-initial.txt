        │       └── InsertSlot (h-1 rounded bg-accent/30) interleaved with children
        ├── FieldBlock (data-layout-node-type=field)
        │   ├── role=group tabIndex=0, rounded-[18px] border, selected: accent shadow
        │   ├── DragHandle (opacity-0 group-hover)
        │   ├── FieldIcon tile (rounded-[10px] bg-bg-default/85)
        │   ├── identity: key + datatype | inline inputs
        │   ├── summary strip: Description / Hint (border-t)
        │   ├── InlineToolbar (dense selects, 11px mono)
        │   └── resize handles (absolute right/bottom)
        └── DisplayBlock — analogous + glyph tile
